# Character & Lore Bible

## Characters
- **Lysandra Eirenaios** — Archaeology student and newly chosen Guardian of the Bridge. Discovers Atlantis in Chapter 1 and leads alliances across chapters 2-5.
- **Seraphel** — Atlantean guide and leader of the Choir, mentors Lysandra in the city's harmonic technology (Chapters 1-2).
- **Arkan** — Former guardian who succumbs to discord and leads the uprising before seeking redemption (Chapters 3-5).
- **Atlantean Choir** — Sentient harmonic collective that maintains the Bridge's resonance (Chapters 1-5).

## Artifacts & Concepts
- **The Bridge** — Resonant gateway linking Atlantis with the surface world; awakened in Chapter 1 and protected throughout the saga.
- **Memory Harp** — Instrument that encodes messages in harmonics, used to recruit surface allies (Chapters 2-3).
- **Harmonic Alliance** — Coalition of surface and Atlantean allies formed to preserve balance and defeat discordant forces (Chapters 3-5).

## Timeline
- **Chapter 1:** Lysandra hears the sea's song and is summoned to Atlantis.
- **Chapter 2:** Atlantis awakens and the Memory Harp reveals hidden histories.
- **Chapter 3:** Surface Accord forged; letters sent to potential allies.
- **Chapter 4:** Discordant Uprising challenges the Accord.
- **Chapter 5:** Final Harmony restores balance and secures the Bridge.
